About me

I have 12 years of experience managing and creating digital projects for Banco de Bogota, Visa, Homecenter Sodimac, Mondelez Latinoamerica, McCann Erickson, Procolombia, and so on. I transform my clients' ideas into tangible and amazing projects that give a competitive advantage. I use a design methodology focus on user experience for web, mobile and app design, ecommerce, and online banking.
